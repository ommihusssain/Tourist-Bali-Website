Essential Travel Tips

  • ○ Stay hydrated and always carry sunscreen.
  • ○ Respect local customs, especially when visiting temples.
  • ○ Use reliable transportation like Bluebird taxis or Grab.
  • ○ Always have some Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) cash for small purchases.
  • ○ Try local food but be cautious of food allergies.
